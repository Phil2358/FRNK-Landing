How it Works

Connect accounts, edit once, sync everywhere — with AI keeping everything consistent.

Connect Your Profiles

Link your social and professional accounts to FRNK in seconds.

FRNK uses secure API connections (OAuth) to connect to your platforms — like LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter/X, TikTok, and more. Once connected, FRNK pulls in your existing bios, headlines, profile pictures, banners, and links. All your profiles appear side-by-side in your dashboard, ready to edit and sync from one place.

Edit Once, Update Everywhere

Make changes once in FRNK, and sync them across all platforms.

Instead of logging into each platform separately, FRNK lets you edit your details — name, bio, job title, profile picture, and links — in a single editor. FRNK adapts the content to match each platform’s style (LinkedIn = professional, Instagram = casual, Twitter = short and sharp). With one click, you can publish everywhere at once or select which platforms to update.

AI-Assisted Consistency

Stay polished with FRNK’s smart AI assistance.

FRNK’s AI doesn’t just copy-paste — it helps you look your best. It checks for inconsistencies (like one profile saying Berlin while another says Corfu), adapts tone for each platform, and even resizes profile images to the right dimensions. You’ll also get gentle reminders when your branding is outdated, so your digital presence always stays fresh and consistent.
