How it Works

1) Create your campaign

Write your master bio and CTA link in FRNK. We generate platform‑specific versions that respect length limits and emoji/formatting rules.

2) Copy + Open

Click a platform button (e.g., Copy + Open Instagram). FRNK auto‑copies the right text and opens that platform’s Edit Profile page. Paste, save, done.

Desktop opens the edit page in a new tab. On mobile, we open the app/profile so you tap “Edit Profile”, paste, and save.

3) Check off & stay aligned

Mark each profile as updated and keep your Consistency Score high. FRNK flags length issues and reminds you when promos expire or bios drift.

4) Track clicks

Your CTA uses a single FRNK link with per‑platform tags. See which bio drives traffic by platform and date range—simple, actionable analytics.
