How it Works

1 · Connect

Link your profiles — LinkedIn, Instagram, X, and more.

FRNK connects via secure OAuth to platforms like LinkedIn, Instagram, X/Twitter, and TikTok. It imports your bios, headlines, profile photos, banners, and links so you can view everything side‑by‑side.

2 · Sync

Update once; FRNK assists and mirrors your identity everywhere.

Edit your name, role, bios, links, and profile images in one place. FRNK adapts tone per platform (LinkedIn = professional, Instagram = casual, X = concise) and publishes everywhere at once or selectively.

3 · Stay Polished

Keep bios, photos, and links aligned across platforms — automatically.

FRNK flags inconsistencies (titles, locations), suggests tone‑appropriate rewrites, resizes images to the right dimensions, and nudges you when details get stale.
