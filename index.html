How it Works

1 · Connect

Link your profiles — LinkedIn, Instagram, X, and more.

FRNK uses secure API connections (OAuth) to connect to your platforms — like LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter/X, TikTok, and more. Once connected, FRNK pulls in your existing bios, headlines, profile pictures, banners, and links. All your profiles appear side-by-side in your dashboard, ready to edit and sync from one place.

2 · Sync

Update once; FRNK assists and mirrors your identity everywhere.

Instead of logging into each platform separately, FRNK lets you edit your details — name, bio, job title, profile picture, and links — in a single editor. FRNK adapts the content to match each platform’s style (LinkedIn = professional, Instagram = casual, Twitter = short and sharp). With one click, you can publish everywhere at once or select which platforms to update.

3 · Stay Polished

Keep bios, photos, and links aligned across platforms — automatically.

FRNK’s AI doesn’t just copy-paste — it helps you look your best. It checks for inconsistencies, adapts tone for each platform, and even resizes profile images to the right dimensions. You’ll also get gentle reminders when your branding is outdated, so your digital presence always stays fresh and consistent.
